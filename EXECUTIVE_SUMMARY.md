## Core Platform: XOPS360

At the heart of Axiomio's offerings is **XOPS360**, an AI-powered cloud optimization platform that unifies DevOps, SecOps, CloudOps, and AI Agents into a single intelligent ecosystem. XOPS360 transforms operational complexity into competitive advantage by providing:

- **AI Cost Optimization Agent**: Conversational AI for instant cost analysis and optimization recommendations
- **Real-Time Cost Intelligence**: Live visibility into cloud spending with proactive anomaly detection
- **Cloud Security Posture Management (CSPM)**: Continuous security monitoring and automated remediation
- **Container Vulnerability Scanning**: Pre-deployment security for AMIs and ECR images
- **Cloud Well-Architected Review AI Module**: Continuous compliance and best practice alignment
- **Unified Asset Management**: Single-pane visibility across all cloud assets and regions
